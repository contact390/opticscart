Eye Care Tips

Wash Your Hands

Always wash and dry hands thoroughly before touching your lenses.

Never Sleep in Lenses

Unless prescribed, remove lenses before sleeping to avoid infections.

Use Fresh Solution

Never reuse old contact lens solution. Clean and rinse properly.

Replace Regularly

Stick to the recommended lens replacement schedule from your optometrist.
