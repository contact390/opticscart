Driving License Classes

Class 2B

Vehicle Type: Motorcycles ≤200cc

Minimum Age: 18 years

Requirements: BTT, RTT, Practical Test

Validity: 1 year (Probationary), then 5 years

Class 3

Vehicle Type: Cars, vans ≤2500kg

Minimum Age: 18 years

Requirements: BTT, FTT, Practical Test

Validity: 5 years

Class 4

Vehicle Type: Heavy vehicles >2500kg

Minimum Age: 21 years

Requirements: Class 3 license + additional test

Validity: 3 years

How to Apply

1

Enroll in Driving School

Register with an approved driving center (CDC, BBDC, or SSDC) or private instructor

2

Pass Theory Tests

Complete Basic Theory Test (BTT) and Final Theory Test (FTT) at the driving center

3

Practical Lessons

Complete mandatory practical lessons (minimum 20-25 sessions depending on school)

4

Final Practical Test

Pass the driving test conducted by Traffic Police at the driving center

License Renewal

Eligibility

  • License must not be expired for more than 3 years
  • No outstanding traffic offenses
  • Medically fit to drive (if age 65+)

Documents Required

  • Current/expired Singapore driving license
  • NRIC or passport
  • Medical certificate (if applicable)

Renewal Methods

  • Online via Police e-Services
  • AXS stations islandwide
  • Traffic Police Headquarters

Important Note:

Driving licenses can be renewed up to 1 year before expiry date. Late renewals may require retaking tests.

Foreign License Conversion

Eligible Countries

Holders of driving licenses from these countries may convert without tests:

Australia
UK
USA
Germany
France

Conversion Process

  1. Obtain official translation of license (if not in English)
  2. Pass Basic Theory Test (BTT) at driving center
  3. Submit application at Traffic Police HQ with:
    • Original license + photocopy
    • NRIC/Passport + photocopy
    • Entry permit/employment pass
    • BTT pass certificate

Processing Time: 1-2 weeks. Fee: $50. Valid for 5 years.
