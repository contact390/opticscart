Frequently Asked Questions – Hitaishi Lens

What are Hitaishi contact lenses made of?

Hitaishi contact lenses are crafted from advanced hydrogel materials that provide comfort, oxygen permeability, and UV protection.

Are Hitaishi lenses suitable for sensitive eyes?

Yes, our lenses are designed to be gentle and irritation-free, especially for users with dry or sensitive eyes.

How long can I wear Hitaishi lenses in a day?

You can comfortably wear them for 10–12 hours a day. However, always follow your eye care professional’s guidance.

Do Hitaishi lenses offer power for both near and far vision?

Yes, we offer single vision, toric, and multifocal lenses to suit different vision correction needs.

Where can I buy Hitaishi contact lenses?

Hitaishi lenses are available at authorized optical retailers and online on our official store locator page.
